History

Realizing that not many know of Malaysia when introducing her home country to her friends and people here in the US, Jia Wern decided to change that and that's when she started the website, My Malaysia. The site was created during her freshman year here at Rutgers and her goal is to introduce and share her home country to her friends and people in the United States who may not be familiar with Malaysia! Since Jia Wern is in the US right now, she has recruited some of her local friends in Malaysia to help out on the site providing accurate and up-to-date information.

Meet the Team!

I hope you enjoy reading my site!

Jia Wern - Author/Creator

Hello! I'm Jia Wern or JW for short and I’m an international student from Malaysia here at Rutgers University! I’m currently a junior majoring in computer science. When someone mention Southeast Asia, the first few countries that comes to mind are Singapore, Vietnam or the Philippines. As the author and creator, I’m hoping that this website would change that and would allow more people to know more about Malaysia, it's culture, background and people!

Hibiscus - Web Designer

I'm the national flower of Malaysia and I enjoy designing things. I love simplicity and I try to add meanings to my designs! I designed the website's backgound color gradient to represent the colors on the national flag of Malaysia! Similarly, both my color and petals also have their own representations. The color red symbolizes the courage, life, and rapid growth of the Malaysian people while my five petals represents the five Rukun Negara of Malaysia.
